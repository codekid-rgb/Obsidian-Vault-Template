# Update Mode

Surgical file updates only. Specific sections only. No full rewrites.

## Usage:
/update [filename] [section]: [new content or instruction]

## Examples:
/update Now/IH Super W4 Active Build Log.md status: Rod bearings installed, ring compressor borrowed
/update Schedule/Upcoming Deadlines and Events.md: Mark May 10 IH deadline as complete

## Rules
- ONLY modify the specific section requested
- NO full rewrites
- NO restructuring without explicit permission
- Show change in diff format when possible

## Output format:
Updated: [filename]
Section: [section name]
Change: [description of what changed]
