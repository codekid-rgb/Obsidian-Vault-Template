# Evening Debrief

Read today's daily note at Daily/2026/YYYY-MM-DD.md. Note what was planned in the morning section.

## Ask the user (one at a time):

1. What did you actually get done today?
2. What didn't happen, and why?
3. Any new developments or things worth noting?
4. Where did you feel God today?
5. One thing you're grateful for.
6. Any friction — something that slowed you down or felt off?
7. What are your top 3 for tomorrow?

## Then output ONLY:

## Evening — [DATE]

**Completed:** [list]

**Not completed:** [list + brief reason if given]

**New developments:** [if any]

**Gratitude:** [what they said]

**Friction:** [what got in the way]

**Tomorrow's top 3:**
1.
2.
3.

## Rules
- Save evening section to today's daily note
- Note screens-off time if they mention it
- Keep output under 300 words
- No advice or commentary unless asked
- If completion rate was low, note it factually — don't comfort or lecture
